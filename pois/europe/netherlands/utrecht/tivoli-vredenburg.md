Five-hall music complex opened in 2014 at the heart of Utrecht, combining the original 1960s Vredenburg building with a striking new structure designed by Hertzberger. The halls range from a 1700-seat main room to intimate 200-seat venues. Programmes everything from symphony orchestras to club nights.
