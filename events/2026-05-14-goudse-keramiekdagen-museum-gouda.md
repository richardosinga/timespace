The annual Gouda Ceramics Days returns to the historic market square, celebrating the city's deep tradition in pottery and ceramic arts. Museum Gouda marks the occasion with a special Verhaal op Zaal programme on 14–15 May, placing the ceramics tradition in its historical context.
