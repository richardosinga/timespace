A late-night musical theatre show hosted at Café Dox inside the Luxor, running every Saturday from 9 May through 6 June 2026. Featuring Elfie Tromp in an intimate pay-what-you-can format, it's one of the most accessible and spontaneous theatre experiences in Rotterdam.
