Uganda's Nyege Nyege collective returns to WORM for a second sold-out-calibre edition, following their acclaimed 2025 appearance. DJ Diaki, Cheb Runner, Shaun D and others deliver high-voltage rhythms and percussion-driven sounds, blending African electronic music with house, drum and bass and global Afro-diasporic styles.
