A guided architecture tour through the Kunsthal's iconic building designed by Rem Koolhaas in 1988–1989, exploring its materials, entrance positioning, and famously steep ramp. Two sessions on this date: 13:00–14:00 and 14:30–15:30, each costing €5 per person; no museum admission ticket required.
