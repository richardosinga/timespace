A partnership between Kunsthal Rotterdam and Haven Hotel combining a guided tour of the Flowers Forever exhibition with a floral cocktail at the hotel. Two slots are offered: 13:30–14:45 and 15:00–16:15 for the museum tour, followed by cocktails at Haven Hotel a short walk away. Tickets cost €29 (€15 for museum card or Rotterdam Pass holders); tours are conducted in Dutch.
