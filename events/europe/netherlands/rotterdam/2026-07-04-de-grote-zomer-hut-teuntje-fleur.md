An interactive summer installation by Rotterdam graphic artist Teuntje Fleur filling the Kunsthal's Auditorium stairs with a colourful fantasy forest of sculptures and discovery boxes centred on a large hut inspired by childhood hideaways. Visitors can freely explore, build felt sculptures, and relax inside the hut; the installation runs from 4 July to 30 August, Tuesday–Sunday 10:00–17:00. Participation is free with a museum ticket; visitors under 19 enter free.
