Every first Sunday of the month, Kunsthal Rotterdam offers guided tours of its exhibitions and the iconic Rem Koolhaas building. On June 7 two slots run back-to-back: 11:00–12:00 and 12:15–13:15, in Dutch, with a KunstHoppen format surveying all current shows. Tickets cost €5 per person on top of museum admission.
