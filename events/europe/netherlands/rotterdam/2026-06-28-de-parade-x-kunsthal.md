A collaboration between Kunsthal Rotterdam and De Parade, the travelling theater festival known for its blend of theater, music, dance and visual art. Two tour slots (13:30–14:50 and 15:00–16:50) combine a guided museum tour with a walk to the Parade grounds and a meet-and-greet with the Candy Girls performers. A children's drawing workshop with poet and illustrator runs alongside the tours; all activities are in Dutch.
