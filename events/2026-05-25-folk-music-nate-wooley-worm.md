Three of the most celebrated American improvisers — trumpet player Nate Wooley, drummer Chris Corsano and percussionist/pianist Ches Smith — perform together at WORM. All three are cornerstones of the contemporary free improvisation scene and this trio combination promises a rare and exciting evening.
