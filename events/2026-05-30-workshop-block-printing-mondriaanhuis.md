A hands-on textile workshop combining a guided tour of the "Orde en Intuïtie" exhibition with a block-printing session at the nearby Katoendrukkerij de Volmolen. Under the guidance of instructor Nathalie Cassée, participants select decorative blocks and print their own take-home cotton fabric design.
