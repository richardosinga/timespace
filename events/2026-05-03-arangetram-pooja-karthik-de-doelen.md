A debut solo performance — the arangetram — of Bharatanatyam dancer Pooja Karthik, marking her formal graduation from years of classical Indian dance training. An arangetram is a milestone ceremony in South Indian classical dance, presenting the student's full repertoire to an audience for the first time.
