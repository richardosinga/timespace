Monthly Film & Filosofie screening of the Oscar-winning Hungarian drama Son of Saul (2015), following a concentration camp prisoner who risks everything to give a Jewish burial to a boy he believes is his son. Part of the May programming block "(de prijs van) Vrijheid" exploring the price of freedom through film and theatre.
