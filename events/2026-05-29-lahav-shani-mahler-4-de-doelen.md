Lahav Shani conducts the Rotterdam Philharmonic Orchestra in Mahler's Fourth Symphony, the most intimate and chamber-like of the composer's major works. Its luminous scoring and the pastoral paradise of the final movement make it one of the most beloved symphonies in the repertoire.
