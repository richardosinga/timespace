The official Queen musical comes to Nieuwe Luxor for a twelve-night run (6–17 May). Set in a dystopian future where authentic rock music is banned, the show features more than twenty Queen classics including Bohemian Rhapsody and Somebody to Love. Approved by Brian May and Roger Taylor.
