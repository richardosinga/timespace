One-off documentary screening exploring how Amersfoort's Stadsring transformed from a pleasant urban park into a highway, produced by the citizen initiative "Van Ring naar Park". After the film, landscape architects Wim Voogt and Jeroen van Sprundel discuss comparable urban greening projects in Utrecht and Breda, followed by an open dialogue with the audience.
