Swedish singer-songwriter Isak Danielson brings his emotionally charged pop to BIRD Rotterdam. Known for nearly a billion Spotify streams, Danielson's music is personal, melodious and direct — with comparisons drawn to Adele, Lewis Capaldi and Sam Smith. He performs new material from under a fresh creative direction. Doors at 20:00.
