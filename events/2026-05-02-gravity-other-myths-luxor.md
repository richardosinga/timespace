Australian circus company Gravity & Other Myths return to Rotterdam with their most ambitious show yet. Ten elite acrobats explore authenticity in a screen-saturated world through human towers, dance and precision acrobatics, set against LED walls and backed by original electronic music. Winner of Best Circus at the Adelaide Fringe 2025. Part of the Circusstad Festival.
