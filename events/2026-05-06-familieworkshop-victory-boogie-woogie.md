A family workshop for children aged 6 and up exploring the connection between music and painting — the same link that inspired Mondrian's final work Victory Boogie Woogie. Participants paint to a changing soundtrack of slow melodies and loud, rhythmic jazz to discover what images different music evokes. Museum admission is included in the €10 ticket price.
