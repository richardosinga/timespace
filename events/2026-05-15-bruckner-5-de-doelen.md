Bruckner's Fifth Symphony is one of his most architecturally ambitious works, a vast contrapuntal cathedral of sound closing with a monumental double fugue. Hearing it live in De Doelen's superb acoustic is an unmissable experience for any admirer of the Romantic orchestral repertoire.
