A unique performance featuring mechanical musical installations by Remko Scha: electrically-powered guitars and bass guitars in frameworks, played by rotating strings, metal brushes and rods attached to fans, drills and circular saws — a band without musicians, but with undeniable energy. Composer and poet Samuel Vriezen, a 2026 nominee for the Great Poetry Prize, presents these instruments in Amersfoort, blurring the boundary between human and machine.
