One-time documentary screening examining the cross-pollination between Dutch and Indonesian architecture across Java, Sumatra, and Dutch cities, tracing how architects adapted to tropical climates and how Indonesian design influenced Dutch buildings. The 72-minute film is introduced by expert Frans Leidelmeijer, specialist in Art Nouveau, Amsterdam School and Dutch-Indies architectural styles.
