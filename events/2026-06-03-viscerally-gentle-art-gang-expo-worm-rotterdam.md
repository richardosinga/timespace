A four-day group exhibition at WORM featuring around 15 artists presenting textile art, installations, photography, paper art and more. Developed over a three-month collaborative residency between Art-Gang and WORM, the show culminates on Friday 5 June with a performance night of dance, poetry and live acts. Open daily 3–8 PM, June 3–6.
