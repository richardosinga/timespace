Scottish smallpipe player Brìghde Chaimbeul joins forces with the Maxwell Quartet for an evening of traditional Scottish music that moves between intimacy and power. Chaimbeul is one of the most exciting voices in contemporary folk, drawing on deep Gaelic roots.
