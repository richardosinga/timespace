A lying-down (ligconcert) concert experience at Museum Gouda, featuring singer-songwriter Rositha Rikkers. Part of Gouda Pop Week, the event invites audiences to lie on the floor and listen to music in total relaxation — an intimate format that makes the museum's historic spaces feel entirely new.
