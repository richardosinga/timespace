An opportunity to meet textile artist Theo Rooden, whose woven works feature in the current exhibition "Orde en Intuïtie" at the Mondriaanhuis. Rooden discusses his creative process — how he balances systematic ordering with intuitive decision-making — and welcomes conversations with visitors about his hand-woven pieces.
